Join Our Team

Find your role. Make your mark.

Security
The backbone of every opening. Check IDs, maintain the door, roam the venue, protect staff and patrons. You're the first face people see and the last line if things go sideways.
Dancer
The core of what makes VV special. Perform on stage, interact with patrons, offer private dances at the Vanilla Unicorn. Courtesy and professionalism at all times. Must cross-train in a secondary role.
Bartender
Keep the drinks flowing and the conversation going. Take orders, learn recipes, work the bar with personality. Step out from behind it when things are slow to offer drinks around the venue.
DJ
Set the mood for the entire venue. Run the sound system, read the room, take requests, and act as the announcer when the energy needs a push. The playlist is yours — keep it appropriate for the location.
